Cold drinks, warm people, and a dog who thinks he owns the place.

We're the bar behind the market in the heart of Tubac. No cover, no attitude, no dress code — just good tequila, $5 drafts, free popcorn, and a patio that's hard to leave once the sun starts going down.

Rhonda built this place to feel like your living room, if your living room had a pool table and a really good Cadillac Margarita.
